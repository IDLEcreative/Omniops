How It Works

  1. Navigate to /dashboard/privacy
  2. Go to the "Audit Log" tab
  3. Click on any audit entry row (they have a hover effect)
  4. The modal opens with full details
  5. Use tabs to switch between Summary and Raw Data views
  6. Click "Copy JSON" or "Download" to export the data
